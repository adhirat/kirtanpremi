Support the Mission

Your seva helps us sustain the transcendental vibrations of the Holy Name and support the global kirtan community.

Spiritual Charity (Seva)

In the Vedic tradition, supporting the propagation of spiritual knowledge is considered the highest form of charity. Your contributions directly enable us to travel, record, and maintain the standards of kirtan worship.

Instrument Maintenance

Sponsor the skinning of mridangas, purchase of bellows for harmoniums, and high-quality bell-metal kartals.

Travelling Sankirtan

Help us take the holy name to remote villages where access to spiritual music is limited.

Transparent Allocation

100% of your donations are used for missionary activities. We provide periodic updates on how your seva is transforming lives.

The Impact of Your Seva

Over the last year, with your support, we have been able to conduct over 150 kirtan events across 5 countries, distributing the seeds of bhakti to thousands. We also started a free training program for young mridanga players in Vrindavan.

"He who distributes the holy names is the most dear servant of the Lord." — Chaitanya Charitamrta
